About Kenyatta University

Kenyatta University is a leading public university in Nairobi, Kenya. It was established in 1965 and is named after Jomo Kenyatta, Kenya's first president. The university offers a wide range of academic programs and has a diverse student population.

Kenyatta University is known for its strong emphasis on research and innovation. It has state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty that is committed to providing quality education and fostering intellectual growth.

The university campus is located in a serene environment, providing a conducive atmosphere for learning and personal development. It offers various extracurricular activities, including sports, clubs, and cultural events, to enhance the overall student experience.

Whether you are interested in pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate studies, Kenyatta University provides a vibrant and supportive community that prepares students for successful careers and lifelong learning.
